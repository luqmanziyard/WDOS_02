Sigiriya

Tales of Sigiriya

The Sigiriya Rock Fortress has a dramatic and thrilling past that is bound to captivate all who visit the site. Towering over its surroundings, this ancient fortress is a fitting background for a tale of epic proportions. Discover a tale of ambition, betrayal and vengeance when you learn about the king who had the vision and perseverance to create this magnificent palace in the sky.

Attraction in Sigiriya

Apart from the main attraction of the Lion Rock, the region of Sigiriya has other significant cultural and religious sites such as Pidurangala and the Dambulla Cave Temples that you can visit. The Minneriya and Kaudulla parks are ideal places to visit for sightings of the majestic Sri Lankan elephant and other endemic animals, reptiles and birds

Experience in Sigiriya

Enjoy a rich cultural and historical experience when you visit the Lion Rock, the Pidurangala Rock and the Dambulla Cave Temple complex during your stay in Sigiriya. Venture out into the Minneriya and Kaudulla National Parks to experience the captivating phenomenon referred to as ‘The Gathering’, where large herds of Sri Lankan elephants congregate at watering holes during the dry season. Float over the charming countryside in a hot air balloon and enjoy a bird’s eye view of the territory.
